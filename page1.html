YUNG YIDDISH           יונג יידיש

Founded in 1993 by Mendy Cahan, YUNG YiDiSH is a non-profit organization that set for itself the purpose of preserving and transmitting Yiddish culture, as well as encouraging contemporary Yiddish creativity in its various forms. The organization’s members and friends consider Yiddish a language alive, an essential ingredient of our cultural heritage to be fostered by looking both back and forward.

ESN TEG!

In the old days, the Ashkenazi tradition of esn teg, eat a day!, provided poor yeshiva students living away from home with meals for the day so that they can devote themselves to studying.

We are asking you to revive the tradition and help Yung Yidish stay alive for a day!
